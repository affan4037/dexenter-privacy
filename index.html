Privacy Policy for DeXenter

Last Updated: June 23, 2025

1. Who We Are

DeXenter is a student-focused social networking platform for students 15+ to connect, share, and engage. For questions: support@dexenter.pk

2. What Data We Collect

  • Google Sign-In (name, email)
  • Profile info (picture, bio)
  • User posts, comments, images
  • Crash & usage logs (Firebase, Sentry)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide core features, enhance security, debug issues, and improve app experience.

4. Data Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We use Firebase, Cloudflare R2, and Sentry as our processors.

5. User Rights

You can view, update, or delete your account anytime. Email us to request data removal.

6. Data Security

Data is encrypted in transit, JWT is used for auth, and we use secure cloud storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

We don’t knowingly allow users under 15. Accounts violating this will be removed.

8. Updates

If we update this policy, you’ll be notified in-app.

9. Contact Us

For privacy issues: dexenter0@gmail.com
